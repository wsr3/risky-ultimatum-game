Thank you for participating in this study. Your final payoff is calculated based on other participants' actions. You will receive your payment within 24 hours after we verify your responses.

Our research intends to explore how the bargain works when uncertainty exists. We are also interested in the behavioral changes in reaction to varying risks. This research helps us better understand some real world questions in politics and business particularly.

For participants assigned as Proposers, we are interested in your offers when facing the uncertainty that your opponent may reject and obtain the right to allocate. Also, the change of behaviors in response to the change of uncertainty is also of interest. For participants assigned as Responders, we want to observe your decision to accept or reject when the uncertainty varies.

We also collected information of your demographic characteristics and risk attitude, these are factors that may be related to your decisions.

We hope you enjoyed the experience of participating!

If you have more questions about this study, please contact Shirui Wang at wsr@iastate.edu; or Elizabeth Hoffman at bhoffman@iastate.edu.
